DEPARTMENT OF BUSINESS AND ACCOUNTANCY

VISION

The Department of Business and Accountancy envisions itself to be one of the leading schools in the field of business education in the region by providing globally competitive and God-fearing professionals and entrepreneurs who will share their resources in uplifting the lives of the needy but deserving students of City College of Calamba.

MISION

The Department of Business and Accountancy commits itself to nurture and develop the potentials of the students to become future Certified Public Accountants, business leaders, and entrepreneurs who are technologically adept, fully aware of global economic conditions, responsibly concerned with the environment, human and natural resources by providing an education that instills the standard of ethical conduct for business and personal dealings and Filipino values as emulated by Dr. Jose Rizal.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Accountancy
  • Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System
