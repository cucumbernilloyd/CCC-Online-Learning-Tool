DEPARTMENT OF ARTS AND SCIENCES

VISION

The Department of Arts and Sciences endeavors itself to be recognized as a globally competitive college, with outstanding faculty, committed creative and contemplative student scholars, who will serve as the center of academic excellence in the region in basic sciences, natural sciences, humanities, arts, researches and technologies.

MISION

The Department of Arts and Sciences of City College of Calamba offers extensive variety of educational opportunities, instilled with a mission to provide knowledge and skills to aspiring professionals in different chosen field with acquired moral and values, leading towards becoming DEDICATED, ADVOCATE, and SENSIBLE individuals.
