Degree Curriculum Overview

Program: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

This is an overview of the curriculum for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program.

Core Courses

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management
  • Software Engineering

Elective Courses

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Web Development
  • Mobile App Development

Capstone Project

Students are required to complete a capstone project in their final year.
